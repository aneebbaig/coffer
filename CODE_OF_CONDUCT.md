# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainers via a private GitHub Security Advisory or by contacting a maintainer directly. Maintainers will review and respond to reports, and may remove, edit, or reject contributions or contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
